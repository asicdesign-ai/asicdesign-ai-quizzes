# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This project is open to contributors. To keep collaboration effective, all participants are expected to follow basic professional standards.

## Guidelines

Contributors should:

- Communicate clearly and respectfully  
- Focus on technical content in discussions  
- Provide constructive feedback when reviewing code or ideas  
- Accept feedback and iterate in good faith  
- Keep discussions relevant to the project  

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks  
- Disruptive or intentionally unproductive behavior  
- Insults or derogatory comments  
- Repeated off-topic or inflammatory discussions  
- Sharing private information without permission  

## Maintainer Rights

Maintainers may remove or modify contributions, comments, or issues if behavior disrupts the project or community.

This includes pull requests, issues, and discussion threads.

## Enforcement

Reports of issues will be handled by maintainers. Actions will be taken at their discretion to maintain project quality and productivity.

## Contact

For concerns, contact the maintainers via the project’s issue tracker or designated communication channel.
